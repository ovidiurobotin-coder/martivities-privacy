    6. User Rights (GDPR Articles 15-22)


    Any user has the right to:


    ✅ Access (Art. 15)

    Request a complete copy of your personal data in JSON/CSV format.

    Timeline: 30 days, free of charge.


    ✅ Rectification (Art. 16)

    Correct inaccurate data in your account (name, email).


    ✅ Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") (Art. 17)

    Request complete deletion of your personal data.

    ⚠️ Exception: Compliance logs are retained 7 years per Romanian law.


    ✅ Restriction of Processing (Art. 18)

    Request us to stop processing your data (e.g., no notifications).


    ✅ Data Portability (Art. 20)

    Your data can be exported in standard format.


    ✅ Objection (Art. 21)

    Refuse analytics, marketing emails anytime.


    ✅ No Automated Decision-Making (Art. 22)

    Martivities does not make automatic decisions affecting you (e.g., ban without human review).
