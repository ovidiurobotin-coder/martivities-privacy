Privacy Policy - Martivities

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Martivities ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our educational application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information.

Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored only on your device using local storage:

  • Child's name and avatar selection
  • Game progress, scores, and stars
  • Language preference
  • Family activity completion data
  • Premium subscription status

Important: All user data is stored ONLY on your device. We do NOT transmit, collect, or store any personal information on external servers.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Martivities is designed for children ages 6-12. We take children's privacy very seriously:

  • We do NOT collect personal information from children
  • We do NOT use advertising or tracking
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • All data remains on the device
  • No account creation is required

In-App Purchases

Martivities offers optional premium subscriptions. All purchases are processed through Google Play and are subject to Google's privacy policy. We do not have access to payment information.

Parents can manage and restrict in-app purchases through Google Play Settings > Parental Controls.

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device, can be deleted by clearing the app data, and can be reset within the app's Settings.

Third-Party Services

Martivities does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Parental Controls

The app includes a "Parent Zone" protected by a math problem verification. Parents can change the child's avatar, view screen time recommendations, reset all progress, and manage language settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
